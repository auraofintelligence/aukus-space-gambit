Early review support

Help test the gambit without turning it into noise.

The highest-value support is careful expertise: the person who can tell us what would have to be true, what has already been ruled out, and what evidence is missing.

Support lanes

The review needs many kinds of grown-up help.

Orbital mechanics reviewer

Delta-v, docking, reboost, disposal orbit, decay and failure-mode review.

Space law and treaty reviewer

Outer Space Treaty, partner consent, liability, registration and governance implications.

AUKUS / defence-transition reviewer

Pillar II relevance, peaceful framing, export-control realities and public-interest boundaries.

Heritage and science reviewer

ISS as science platform, museum, public artefact, education resource or future research node.

Cost and procurement reviewer

Lifecycle cost comparison, schedule realism, procurement path and who would pay for what.

Communications support

Plain-English explainers, diagrams, corrections, public Q&A and media discipline.
